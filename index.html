Pong

General Rule
  • The objective of the Pong game is to score points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
  • The game begins with the ball in the center of the screen.
  • A point is scored when the ball passes beyond a playerâ€™s paddle and reaches the edge of the screen behind them.
  • The game restarts with the ball in the center after each point is scored.
  • The game continues until a player reaches the predetermined number of points.
How to play
  • Move your mouse left or right to control the horizontal movement of your paddle.
  • When the ball makes contact with your paddle, it will bounce back into the play area.
  • The player who reaches the 10 number of points first wins the game.
